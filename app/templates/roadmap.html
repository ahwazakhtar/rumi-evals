Headline — Step 3a: the 0.70 reliability bar is currently unreachable by construction

The paired study resolved the human-vs-AI pairing gap (G1), and it surfaced the real crux: the human coaches barely agree with each other (median inter-coach Fleiss κ ≈ 0.068, near chance). No model can match a “human consensus” better than the humans match themselves — so a failing kappa is as much a rater-calibration / rubric-clarity problem as a model problem. Separately, every model scores systematically harsher than human coaches. See the full Step 3a breakdown →
